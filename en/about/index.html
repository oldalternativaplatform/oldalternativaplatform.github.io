We are Russian company developing and supporting technical solutions for multiplayer browser games.

Our proprietary Flash-3D-engine, physics engine, GUI and high-performance server technology allow us to create realy unique browser games, applications for social networks, promo-projects and showcases.

One of our key projects is multiplayer browser online real-time action game Tanki Online . Also we work on other online games as a developer or as a technological partner.

Our current project is the second generation of Tanki Online optimized for social networks and mobile devices. At the same time we work on such social games as Aqua3D (in association with iHeroes company) and Russian Roulette (in association with Arvara company). One of "big" current browser projects is Metro 2033 Online.

Browser applications based on Adobe Flash are our forte. We have been working with this platform more then 10 years and our solutions are always on the forefront of technology. Closely cooperating with Adobe company and working in pre-release groups, we actively participate in developing of new Flash Player versions. Also we regularly take part in Adobe MAX conferences as reporters.

Our headquarter is allocated in Perm and consists of 30 employees. We also created AlternativaChina company in Shanghai which adapts and operates our projects on Chinese market. Chinese version of Tanki Online was launched in August of 2010.

We sincerely believe all serious applications and games will be in a browser soon and we do our best to make theirs "migration" easier and faster.
